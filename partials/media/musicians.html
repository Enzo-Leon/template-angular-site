DJ Alkaloid

Alkaloid, formerly a member of a breakcore collective called Helter Skeletor, is a DJ act focusing heavily on bass leaden breakbeats, trip hop, UK bass and drum-and-bass tracks. He brings to the decks a pair of ears that have been grooving to electronic beats since a young age and a passion for broken beats, his track selection is sure to include crisp, crunchy drums and wonky bass.
